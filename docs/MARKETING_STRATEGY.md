## Executive Summary

PROVENIQ Origins is positioned to become the **TikTok of memoir creation**—a platform where capturing and sharing life stories is as effortless and viral as posting a 60-second video. This strategy leverages emotional resonance, network effects, and AI-powered features to achieve organic growth while building a defensible moat through irreplaceable personal data.

**Core Thesis:** Dead memories become live, shareable, inheritable assets. The switching cost isn't technical—it's emotional. Once grandma's voice is here, you never leave.

## 3. Go-To-Market Strategy

### 3.1 Phase 1: Seed & Validate (Months 1-3)

**Objective:** Achieve product-market fit with 1,000 passionate users

**Tactics:**
1. **Founder-Led Sales**
   - Personal outreach to 100 families
   - Offer free "Legacy Recording Sessions" 
   - Document and share their stories (with permission)

2. **Content Seeding**
   - Create 10 "Origin Stories" from beta users
   - Post Story Clips to TikTok/Instagram/YouTube Shorts
   - Target emotional virality: tears, laughter, connection

3. **Community Building**
   - Launch private Facebook group: "Origins Storytellers"
   - Weekly "Memory Monday" prompts
   - User-generated content sharing

**Success Metrics:**
- 1,000 registered users
- 100 completed memoirs
- 50 Story Clips shared externally
- NPS > 70

### 3.2 Phase 2: Growth Engine (Months 4-9)

**Objective:** Achieve viral coefficient > 1.0

**Tactics:**
1. **Viral Loop Activation**
   - Family Remix invitations (1 user → 5-10 family members)
   - Story Clip social sharing with watermark/CTA
   - "Gift a Memoir" feature for holidays

2. **Influencer Partnerships**
   - Partner with family/lifestyle YouTubers
   - Sponsor "Interview Your Grandparents" challenge
   - Collaborate with genealogy influencers

3. **Content Marketing**
   - SEO-optimized blog: "How to Write a Memoir"
   - YouTube series: "Stories Worth Telling"
   - Podcast: "The Origins Podcast" — interviews with interesting people about their life stories

4. **Paid Acquisition (Test)**
   - Facebook/Instagram: Target "interested in genealogy, family history"
   - YouTube pre-roll on memoir/family content
   - Retargeting Story Clip viewers

**Success Metrics:**
- 10,000 registered users
- Viral coefficient > 1.0
- CAC < $20
- 500 Story Clips shared/month

### 3.3 Phase 3: Scale & Monetize (Months 10-18)

**Objective:** Achieve $100K MRR

**Tactics:**
1. **Conversion Optimization**
   - Free tier limitations that drive upgrades
   - "Your memoir is 80% complete—upgrade to finish"
   - Voice cloning as premium upsell

2. **Channel Expansion**
   - Affiliate program for estate planners
   - White-label for funeral homes
   - Enterprise for senior living communities

3. **Product-Led Growth**
   - Public memoir profiles (opt-in)
   - "Featured Origins" showcase
   - Annual "Origins Awards" for best memoirs

4. **Paid Acquisition (Scale)**
   - Increase spend on proven channels
   - TV/streaming ads during family programming
   - Direct mail to 55+ demographics

**Success Metrics:**
- 100,000 registered users
- $100K MRR
- 30% free-to-paid conversion
- LTV:CAC > 3:1

## 5. Viral Mechanics & Growth Loops

### 5.1 Primary Viral Loop: Family Remix

```
User creates memory → Invites family to add perspective → 
Family member signs up → Creates their own memories → 
Invites more family → Loop continues
```

**Optimization Levers:**
- Reduce friction in invite flow
- Pre-populate invite message
- Show "perspectives needed" prompts
- Gamify family participation

**Target Metrics:**
- Invite rate: 40% of users invite family
- Acceptance rate: 60% of invites accepted
- Viral coefficient: 1.2-1.5

### 5.2 Secondary Viral Loop: Story Clips

```
User creates Story Clip → Shares to social media → 
Viewer sees watermark/CTA → Clicks through → Signs up → 
Creates their own Story Clip → Loop continues
```

**Optimization Levers:**
- Make clips emotionally compelling
- Optimize watermark placement
- A/B test CTAs
- Track attribution through unique links

**Target Metrics:**
- Clip creation rate: 20% of active users
- Share rate: 50% of clips shared
- Click-through rate: 2-5%
- Conversion rate: 10-20% of clicks

### 5.3 Tertiary Viral Loop: Gift a Memoir

```
User completes memoir → Gifts to family member → 
Recipient receives physical book → Inspired to create own → 
Signs up → Loop continues
```

**Optimization Levers:**
- Holiday gift campaigns
- "Gift subscription" option
- Include signup card in physical books
- QR code to digital version

## 10. Risk Mitigation

### 10.1 Potential Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low viral coefficient | Medium | High | A/B test invite flows, incentivize sharing |
| High CAC | Medium | High | Focus on organic, optimize paid |
| Privacy concerns | Low | High | Transparent policies, consent flows |
| Platform dependency | Medium | Medium | Diversify channels, build email list |
| Competitor response | Medium | Low | Speed to market, feature moat |

### 10.2 Contingency Plans

**If CAC > $50:**
- Pause paid acquisition
- Double down on organic/viral
- Implement referral incentives
- Explore B2B channels

**If Viral Coefficient < 0.5:**
- Redesign invite flow
- Add incentives for invites
- Create "family challenges"
- Focus on single-user value prop

**If Retention < 20% D30:**
- Improve onboarding
- Add engagement features (streaks, prompts)
- Implement push notifications
- Create re-engagement campaigns

- Target non-Ancestry users

- Build community/brand loyalty
- Consider acquisition positioning
- Focus on emotional switching cost
